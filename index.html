Ghost Hunters

Ghost Hunters is a 3D horror game. You will be in a closed church. Find out a way out and kill the evil there.
Ghost Hunters will allow up to 4 players online co-op against the ghost.Stay alive, help your friends. Use all items you have to find and destory the bound items.
In the sigle player, you can have a tutorial to help you get familiar with the game. For VR players, you will find a more realistic gaming experience in VR mode.

Ghost Hunters is powered by Unity.
